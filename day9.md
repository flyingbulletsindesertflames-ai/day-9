learning:NutriTrack, a comprehensive nutrition tracking and health analytics dashboard designed to help users make smarter dietary decisions through data-driven insights.

🚀 Key Features
✅ Personalized User Profile
Tracks age, weight, height, BMI, activity level, and fitness goals.
Provides instant health status indicators and BMI classification.

✅ Advanced Nutrition Dashboard
Real-time monitoring of calories, protein, carbohydrates, and fats.
Visual analytics using interactive charts and progress indicators.
Daily intake compared against recommended nutritional targets.

✅ Smart Nutrient Analysis
Identifies nutrient deficiencies and excesses automatically.
Highlights critical vitamins and minerals requiring attention.
Generates actionable recommendations to improve dietary balance.

✅ Food Logging System
Easy meal tracking with quantity and serving-unit management.
Supports breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snack categorization.
Custom food entry and bulk CSV/XLSX import capabilities.

✅ AI-Powered Meal Planning
Generates structured meal plans based on:
Fitness goals
Dietary preferences
Nutritional requirements
Provides nutritional reasoning behind every meal recommendation.

✅ Comprehensive Health Reports
Detailed daily nutrition summaries.
Intake vs target comparisons.

Exportable PDF reports for tracking long-term progress.

📈 What Makes It Valuable?
Helps users understand not just how much they eat, but what nutrients they are missing.
Bridges the gap between calorie counting and true nutritional wellness.
Supports healthier lifestyle choices through personalized recommendations.
